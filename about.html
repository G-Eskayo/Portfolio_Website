03 : About

My name is Gil Brandon Eskayo, a Junior at the University of Colorado Boulder, majoring in Computer Science with a focus on networking and a burning passion for cybersecurity, particularly in red team environments. Welcome to my portfolio website, a digital canvas where I paint a picture of my skills, projects, and the unique journey that brings me here today.

My story isn't just about a student persuing a prestigious degree; it's about a journey shaped by diverse experiences and a relentless drive to excel. From my early days in Aerospace Engineering to my current pursuit in Computer Science, my path has been anything but conventional. It was during a six-month hiatus for basic training in the Army Reserves that I discovered my one of my callings for leadership, it was in my intro to cyber security course that I found my professional calling. The discipline and resilience I honed in the military now fuel my academic and professional endeavors.

I am an Eagle Scout, a title that speaks to my commitment to leadership and community service. My knack for leadership was further honed as the President of the Computer Automation and Design club at my community college, where we competed against giants like UCLA and Cal Poly. These experiences, combined with my active participation in the Cybersecurity Club and University of Colorado Bug Bounty Collective, have shaped me into a well-rounded individual with a deep understanding of teamwork and innovation.

But what truly sets me apart as a candidate? It's my insatiable curiosity and dedication to lifelong learning. Whether it's mastering Python, exploring the intricacies of backend design, or even perfecting a new coffee brewing technique, I am always expanding my horizons. This thirst for knowledge is not just confined to academics; it extends to personal projects like building a biodiesel producer from scratch in high school, a testament to my hands-on approach and problem-solving skills.

My favorite class, "Intro to Cybersecurity in a Converging World," wasn't just another course; it was a revelation. It set me on a definitive path in Computer Science, aligning perfectly with my love for seeing immediate results from my creations. The joy of solving complex problems and the satisfaction of a well-executed program are what drive me in this field.

My "superpower" lies in my ability to view challenges from multiple perspectives, play devil's advocate for the greater good, and navigate bureaucratic processes with ease. At 23, I've already dealt with a decade of such challenges, and I thrive on getting things done efficiently and effectively.

What makes me unique as an individual is the resilience and drive instilled in me by my mother. My mother, a single immigrant woman who lost her hearing in her 20s, thrived in the male-dominated field of computer science and still does to this day. She taught me to value passion, perseverance, and relentless pursuit of my goals. Her story is my beacon, guiding me to overcome any adversity and to relentlessly pursue my passions.

So, why should you hire me over another CS majors? Because I am not just a hard worker and a life-long learner; I am someone who is deeply committed to making a difference. I am driven to grow, lead, and excel, not just for personal gain, but to honor the sacrifices and lessons of my parents. My commitment to my work is unwavering, whether it's revisiting my biodiesel project or diving deep into cybersecurity. I am not just looking for a job; I am seeking a platform to make a meaningful impact.

As you navigate through my portfolio, you'll see not just the projects and achievements, but the story of a person who is constantly evolving, learning, and striving to make a difference. I am Gil Brandon Eskayo, and I am ready to bring my unique blend of skills, experience, and passion to your team.
